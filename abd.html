Social Skateboarding Platform

Already Been Done, or ABD for short, tackles a problem that my friends and I had as kids of finding skate spots you would only see in skate videos and magazines. ABD solves that problem by allowing skaters to not only share those skate spots, but any apot they like to skate everyday. Not only can skaters share skate spots, but they will also have the ability to upload videos and photos of the tricks they landed at those spots to claim that it has "already been done"

Easily create skate spots and share it with a community of skaters. Skaters can browse through a collection of skate spots and search for specific skate spots in their area.

Already create a skate spot or found a skate spot you want to skate? Go ahead and land the gnarliest trick you can and post it to the skate spot. Keep an eye out for what everyone else is landing at that spot as well.

Support other skaters by checking out their photos or clips and make sure to leave a comment to cheer them on.
